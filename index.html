The Flame Sword of St.Paul

from heaven a voice
cried: "rise, Paul, and fight!"
and from the clouds fell a
sword - a flame that never
lies.
seven knights of darkness
rose,
the night trembled as paul cried out:
"the light of heaven shall
not fade!"

Dark knights fight

a fiery strike split the
shadow,
the knights fell, and sin with
them.
when the last turned into
dust,
the sword became pure light
and paul walked on with
faith as his armor.

The end of the
story of saint
Paul: the fiery
sword
